Where to Go

Top Travel
Destinations in Kenya

Nine remarkable places — from world-famous game reserves to UNESCO heritage coastlines.

Maasai Mara

Narok
Wildlife Safari & Great Migration

Famous for the annual wildebeest migration and abundant wildlife including all of Africa's Big Five.

Best time: July – October

Diani Beach

Coast
White Sand Beaches & Water Sports

Crystal-clear waters and a relaxed beach atmosphere make Diani one of East Africa's finest coastal escapes.

Best time: December – March

Mount Kenya

Central
Hiking & Scenic Views

A popular trekking destination offering stunning alpine landscapes and Africa's second-highest summit.

Best time: January – February

Lake Nakuru

Rift Valley
Flamingos & Rhino Sanctuary

Home to vast flocks of flamingos and one of Kenya's key rhino conservation parks.

Best time: Year-round

Amboseli National Park

Kajiado
Elephants & Kilimanjaro Views

Offers iconic views of Mount Kilimanjaro across the border and some of Kenya's largest elephant herds.

Best time: June – October

Lamu Island

Coast
Swahili Culture & History

A UNESCO World Heritage site rich in Swahili architecture, dhow sailing, and centuries of coastal culture.

Best time: November – March

Tsavo National Park

Eastern
Vast Wildlife Reserve

One of the largest parks in Africa, with dramatic red-dust landscapes, diverse ecosystems, and prolific wildlife.

Best time: May – October

Hell's Gate

Naivasha
Hiking & Rock Climbing

Unique gorge park where visitors can walk or cycle among wildlife — no vehicle required.

Best time: Year-round

Mount Longonot

Rift Valley
Volcanic Hiking Trail

A dormant volcano offering a scenic crater rim hike and sweeping panoramic views of the Great Rift Valley.

Best time: Year-round
